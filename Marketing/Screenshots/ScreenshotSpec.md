## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: #00C1FF (Accent Blue)
- Background: #0A0A1E (Midnight)
- Text: #FFFFFF (White)
- Secondary: #4A4A6A (Fog)

### Typography
- Headlines: SF Pro Display Bold
- Body: SF Pro Text Regular
- Captions: SF Pro Text Medium

### Badge Styles
- Shape: Rounded rectangle (8px radius)
- Color: Accent Blue with 90% opacity
- Text: White, SF Pro Text Semibold, 14pt

### Frame Style
- Optional device frame (iPhone 15 Pro)
- Gradient background (Midnight to Deep Blue)
- Subtle shadow (4px blur, 20% opacity)

### Caption Style
- Position: Below screenshot or overlaid at bottom
- Background: Semi-transparent dark gradient
- Text: White, SF Pro Display Medium, 24pt
- Max: 30 characters

- Ensure text is readable at 1x display
- Test color contrast ratios
- Include alt text in metadata
