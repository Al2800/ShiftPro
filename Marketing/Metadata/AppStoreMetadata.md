### Full Description

**ShiftPro - The Professional Shift Tracker for Police Officers & Shift Workers**

Take control of your work schedule with ShiftPro, the comprehensive shift management app designed specifically for police officers and public safety personnel.

**KEY FEATURES**

**Shift Management**
- Log shifts with one tap
- Track scheduled vs. actual hours
- Record breaks and overtime
- Add notes and location details

**Smart Patterns**
- Pre-built police rotation patterns (4-on/4-off, Pitman, 12-hour)
- Create custom weekly or rotating schedules
- Automatic shift generation from patterns
- Flexible pattern editing

**Hours Tracking**
- Real-time hours calculation
- Rate multiplier support (1.0x, 1.3x, 1.5x, 2.0x)
- Pay period tracking and summaries
- Overtime forecasting and warnings

**Calendar Integration**
- Two-way sync with iOS Calendar
- Automatic event creation
- Conflict detection and resolution
- Share your schedule with family

**Dashboard & Insights**
- At-a-glance shift status
- Pay period progress tracking
- Hours trend visualization
- Quick action buttons

**Data Control**
- Export to CSV, PDF, or JSON
- iCloud backup and sync
- Privacy-focused local storage
- Biometric security option

**BUILT FOR PROFESSIONALS**

ShiftPro understands the unique needs of shift workers:
- Night shift friendly dark mode
- Quick shift start/end buttons
- Offline-first design
- Reliable calculations you can trust

**PREMIUM FEATURES**

Upgrade to ShiftPro Premium for:
- Unlimited shift history
- Advanced rotation patterns
- Full calendar sync
- All export formats
- Priority support

**PRIVACY FIRST**

Your data stays on your device. We don't track you, sell your data, or require accounts. iCloud sync is optional and encrypted.

**SUPPORT**

Questions? Contact us at support@shiftpro.app

Download ShiftPro today and take control of your work schedule!
