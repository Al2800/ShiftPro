## Production Notes

### Transitions
- Use smooth, professional transitions
- Match iOS system animation speeds
- Avoid jarring cuts or effects

### Text Treatment
- Font: SF Pro Display Semibold
- Size: 60pt (scaled for device)
- Color: White with subtle shadow
- Duration: 2-3 seconds per text block

### Music Requirements
- Upbeat, professional tone
- No lyrics
- Royalty-free or licensed
- Suggested: Minimal electronic / corporate

### Recording Tips
1. Use Screen Recording with clean demo data
2. Ensure steady touch indicators
3. Keep interactions natural speed
4. Remove any personal/sensitive data
5. Test on actual device for authentic feel

### Device Framing
- Optional: iPhone 15 Pro frame
- Clean, white or dark background
- Consistent throughout video
