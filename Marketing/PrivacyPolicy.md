# Privacy Policy for ShiftPro

**Last Updated:** December 2025

## Introduction

ShiftPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ShiftPro mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

We may collect information you provide directly to us when using the App:

- **Profile Information**: Badge number (optional), department name, rank, employment start date
- **Shift Data**: Work schedules, shift times, break durations, overtime records
- **Pay Information**: Base hourly rate, pay period configuration, rate multipliers
- **Preferences**: Notification settings, calendar sync preferences, display preferences

### Information Automatically Collected

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
- **Performance Data**: App crashes, error logs, performance metrics

### Calendar Data

If you enable calendar synchronization:

- We access your device's calendar to sync shift events
- Calendar data is processed locally on your device
- We do not transmit your calendar data to external servers

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Calculate work hours, overtime, and estimated pay
- Synchronize with your device calendar
- Send notifications about upcoming shifts and reminders
- Improve the App's features and user experience
- Provide customer support
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All shift and personal data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- Data is encrypted at rest using iOS device encryption
- No personal data is transmitted to external servers without your explicit consent

### CloudKit Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:

- Data is synchronized via Apple's CloudKit service
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Apple's privacy policies apply to CloudKit data
- You can disable sync at any time in Settings

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Biometric authentication option (Face ID/Touch ID)
- Secure Keychain storage for sensitive data
- Regular security audits and updates

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information in the following circumstances:

- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing
- **Service Providers**: With Apple for CloudKit sync (if enabled)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or legal process
- **Safety**: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Data Access and Export

- Export all your shift data via the App's export feature
- Request a copy of your data by contacting us

### Data Deletion

- Delete your data through the App's settings
- Data is permanently removed from your device
- CloudKit data is deleted when you sign out

### Opt-Out Options

- Disable notifications in device settings
- Turn off calendar synchronization
- Disable CloudKit sync
- Delete your profile and start fresh

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending a notification for significant changes

## Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with third-party services:

- **Apple Calendar (EventKit)**: For shift synchronization
- **Apple CloudKit**: For optional iCloud sync
- **Apple StoreKit**: For in-app purchases
- **Apple Notifications**: For shift reminders

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs its use.

## California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal data
- Right to opt-out of data sales (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination

## International Users

The App is primarily designed for users in the United States and United Kingdom. If you access the App from other regions, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in accordance with applicable local laws.
